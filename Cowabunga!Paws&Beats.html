Participants:
Fullsuiters, partial-suiters, cosplayers, and non-suiters are all warmly welcome. Participation is open to guests aged 18 and above.

Respect & Consent:
Please respect other attendees and their personal boundaries. Always ask before touching someone, their fursuit, costume, or personal belongings. A “no” always means no.

Allowed props:
Props and small plushies that do not disturb other participants (estimated up to 40 cm, but we won’t be measuring – no worries :D) are allowed. Please leave larger plushies and props at home.

Not allowed:
Sex toys, sharp or pointed parts of fursuits/cosplays that could injure others, paints or other materials that could stain or dirty other participants, and personal drinks. Smoking is prohibited inside the dance hall and is only allowed outside in designated areas.

Photos & Videos:
Photos and videos are only allowed in the dance hall and outdoor areas. Photography or filming is not permitted in the changing lounge or restrooms.

Behavior:
If someone behaves inappropriately or is excessively intoxicated, they may be removed from the event without a refund of the ticket price.

Additional Information:
Please do not enter any neighboring gardens or use any items or facilities located in them. Our designated areas are: the restrooms, the dance hall, the Fursuit Lounge (for fursuiters and their helpers only), and the outdoor area belonging to the dance hall. Anyone who violates this rule will be removed from the event without a refund of the ticket price. They may also be held responsible for any costs caused by their actions, such as damage to gardens, property, or other belongings.

If something is accidentally damaged during the event, please inform a staff member. We will take care of the situation and help resolve the issue. Staff members will be introduced at the beginning of the event and will be clearly visible and easy to recognize throughout the event. Accidents happen ;)

Lost & Found:
Please take care of your personal belongings. The organizers are not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged items. Found items will be collected and stored by the staff.

Tickets & Payment:
Tickets must be purchased and paid for in advance. There will be a very limited number of tickets available at the door, and only standard tickets will be available for on-site purchase.

Payment can be made via bank transfer or PayPal. If you live nearby, cash payment is also possible (with a receipt, by prior arrangement). Once a ticket has been purchased, it is non-refundable. Transferring a ticket to another person is allowed, but it must be confirmed via email (dominika.lenska@web.de) or Telegram (@BorysTheCalf), with confirmation from both the original ticket holder and the new attendee.

Food & Drinks:
Bringing your own food and snacks is allowed with prior approval. Some furries have allergies, and we want to make sure that nobody ends up in the hospital because of an unexpected reaction. Bringing your own drinks is not allowed. There will be a bar available at the event, and free water will be provided at the bar and in the Fursuit Lounge.

A drink menu will be published shortly before the event, together with the food menu, on the website and in the Telegram channel. We will have something for everyone!
